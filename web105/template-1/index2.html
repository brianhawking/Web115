Teacher

Hi, my name is Brian Veitch. I am a math instructor at Northern Illinois University and McHenry County College. At NIU I am 1 the director of the department's Mathematics Assistance Center. I primarily teach our calculus sequence as well as coordinate the finite mathematics course. At MCC I usually teach statistics courses.

I've also done some additional teaching outside the classroom. A few years ago, I helped run a summer math camp where I taught them about encryption and code-breaking. I also worked with NIU Outreach with a program called Stem Read. I showed them how mathematics, puzzles, and encryption were used. The first one I did was Maze Runner. I designed an activity about code-breaking. And showed them how you can overlay images (such as mazes) to form words or codes.
